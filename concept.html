Overview

Think crypto pools, but for AI training resources. A requester funds a bounty and defines a goal epoch. Contributors provide resources that are actually used during training/evaluation. When the epoch ends, we split the bounty among contributors in proportion to how many epochs their resources were used. The platform takes a 1.5% fee.

How it Works

  1. Post a bounty: requester sets bounty B, goal epoch (end time/height), scope, and rules. Funds are escrowed.
  2. Contribute resources: datasets, evals, labeling, or compute. Register artifacts with hashes & licensing.
  3. Log usage per epoch: trainer logs which resource IDs are used each epoch (or fractional share within an epoch).
  4. Close & settle: at epoch end, we compute credits and split payouts automatically.

Epoch‑Share Payouts

Fee: f = 1.5%. Net pool: B_net = B × (1 − f).

Epoch credits: each contributor i earns e_i credits (epochs used). Total credits E = Σ e_i.

Payout: if E > 0, then payout_i = B_net × (e_i / E). Else 0 or refund per rules.

Example: B = $10,000 → fee = $150 → B_net = $9,850. Alice e=99, Bob e=1 → E=100 → Alice $9,751.50, Bob $98.50.

Epoch definition: one training epoch or a fixed step window. Optionally allow fractional credits within each epoch proportional to usage (e.g., dataset sampled 12% of batch → 0.12 credit).

Rounding: round to cents; distribute remainder by largest fractional parts.

Roles & Rules

  • Requester: funds bounty, Gives Model, Set Epoch goal
  • Contributors: Gives Resources to Train Model
  • Platform: escrows funds, computes credits, applies the 1.5% fee, and pays out.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Self‑serve bounty posting, contributor Database, automated usage ingestion.
  • Phase 2: Payout depending on user contribution.

FAQ

What if The goal isn't fulfilled? If total credits E=0, I will fully refund. Or a percentage is taken from the bounty (Depending on the goal percentage) and split.

Can credits be fractional? Yes—usage within an epoch can Depend on contribution/time share.

How is the 1.5% fee applied? Fee is taken from the gross bounty; remaining net is split by credits.

When Will this release? I want to have a fully functional system that works, then get the domain and release it, so maybe a long time, maybe a short time.

How can I support? I might launch a Kickstarter, but you can leave a suggestion on what I should do!
